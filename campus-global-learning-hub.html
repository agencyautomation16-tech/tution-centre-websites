About the Academy

Helping students learn with clarity and confidence

Campus Global Learning Hub is a tuition centre located in Sector A, Bahria Enclave, Islamabad. We support school and college students with structured after-school tuition that focuses on understanding — not memorising.

  • ✓ Clear, step-by-step explanation of concepts in every subject
  • ✓ Exam-focused preparation with regular tests and practice
  • ✓ Personal attention so weaker areas are identified early
  • ✓ Confidence building through encouragement and steady progress
  • ✓ Regular communication with parents about performance

A focused place to study, close to home

Conveniently located in the basement of Dubai Islamic Bank, Sector A, Bahria Enclave — easy for parents to drop off and pick up. Classes run from 12 PM to 9 PM, so students can join after school at a time that suits them.
